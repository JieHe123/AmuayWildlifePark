Amuay Wildlife Park - Information Center

  • Open hours: 8 am to 5 pm, Monday to Sunday.
  • Covid rules: Under the latest rule of government, fully vaccinated guests are no longer to wear masks when visiting Amuay wildlife park.
  • Pets: Currently pets are not allowed for your animal’s safety and ours.
  • Grill: Grills are not allowed to use in any area of the park or within the picnic shelter.
  • Smoking: Smoking is not permitted anywhere on park grounds, including inside vehicles.
  • Campgrounds: Camp is not allowed in the park for your safety.

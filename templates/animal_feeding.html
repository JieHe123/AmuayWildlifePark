Precautions for feeding animals

Feeding animals is great fun, but there are a few things to keep in mind to avoid hurting the animals and you.

1. The food you feed the animals must be the food specified on the sign and do not feed other items at random

2. Please avoid close contact with animals with red tags around their necks

3. Please do not hit the animals or throw rubbish or other objects at thems

4. Parents should keep an eye on their children so that they do not fall into the animals and cause injury

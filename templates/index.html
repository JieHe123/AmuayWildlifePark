Get ready for an adventure

Welcome to Amuay wildlife park. Surround yourself with 72 km2 genuine natural splendour in the west of the Venezuelan capital and meet a wide range of animals and plants through varying grassland, hills, and marshes. Every day in Amuay is unique as large herds of animals roam here. From manatees, Orinoco crocodiles to Venezuelan troupial, thousands of creatures are missing you.
